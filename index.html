About Us

Welcome to Living Parallax, where visual storytelling meets cutting-edge design. We believe in the power of images to inspire, captivate, and transport viewers to extraordinary places.

Our mission is to showcase the world's most breathtaking landscapes and moments through an immersive, interactive experience. Each image is carefully curated to evoke emotion and wonder, utilizing parallax scrolling technology to create depth and movement that brings photography to life.

Every scroll reveals new perspectives, every movement adds dimension, and every image tells a story waiting to be discovered.

Take control of your time with TimerMo today. Bring your designs to life with VectorSticker resources.

Living Parallax is a free HTML template designed by Tooplate for creating stunning fullscreen image galleries.

UI toggle button to hide/show all interface elements for distraction-free viewing

Keyboard navigation (Arrow keys for slides, H key for UI toggle)

Auto-play controls with play/pause functionality

Smooth overlay panels for About, Testimonials, and Contact sections

Beautiful stock photography provided by Unsplash, enhanced with interactive parallax effects and customizable slideshow timing.

Privacy Policy

At Parallax Gallery, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website.

Information We Collect: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and any other details you provide when contacting us through our website forms. We also collect non-personal information such as browser type, IP address, and pages visited for analytics purposes.

How We Use Your Information: The information we collect is used to respond to your inquiries, improve our website experience, and send occasional updates about our gallery (only if you've opted in). We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Data Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. All form submissions are transmitted securely.

Cookies: Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience and track website analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect certain website functionalities.

Third-Party Services: We use Unsplash for image hosting, which may have its own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their terms if you have concerns about image content.

Your Rights: You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us through our contact form.

Changes to This Policy: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
